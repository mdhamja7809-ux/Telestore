Connect your Telegram Storage

TeleStore uses a Telegram Bot as your personal cloud backend.
Your files are stored privately in your Telegram chat.

1 Open Telegram, message @BotFather and send /newbot to create a bot. Copy the Bot Token.
2 Send any message to your new bot, then open: api.telegram.org/bot<TOKEN>/getUpdates to get your Chat ID.
3 Paste both below. Your files will be uploaded to that chat as Telegram documents.
